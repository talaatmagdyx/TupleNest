Priorities, not promises

Where TupleNest
is going.

The roadmap reflects current priorities, not fixed commitments. Quality and safety come before feature count.

Product principles

How we decide what to build

PostgreSQL-first

Depth for one engine beats shallow support for ten.

Safety before convenience

A feature that makes accidents easier doesn't ship.

Local-first

Your data and credentials stay on your machine.

Native performance

A Rust core, bounded memory, fast startup.

Honest claims

If it isn't built and tested, it's marked planned.

Open development

Built in public, decided in the open.

Shipped

Done Already in your hands

Not planned

What TupleNest won't become

Saying no keeps the product focused. These are deliberate non-goals.

  • A full multi-engine database administration suite
  • Support for every database engine
  • Cloud-hosted query execution
  • Advertising-based monetization
  • Mandatory accounts
  • Telemetry or analytics of any kind
No AI assistant is promised. If one is ever built, it will appear here as planned first — not marketed before it exists.
